History

In the early days of 1995, our founders, Rowan Swift and Kayleigh Current, found themselves caught in the eddy of their passion for whitewater rafting. With a shared dream to create a family-focused rafting experience, they set out to build a company that would make a splash in the world of adventure sports.

Pooling their resources, Rowan and Kayleigh launched "Rapid Bonds Whitewater Rafting," making waves in the industry with their unique blend of family-friendly fun and adrenaline-pumping thrills. They built their team with a crew of buoyant, seasoned rafting guides who were all oar-struck by their love for the river and the excitement of navigating its rapids.

Over the years, the company has continued to flourish, providing unforgettable river escapades for thousands of families. By remaining anchored in their values of safety, camaraderie, and unbridled adventure, Rapid Bonds has become a trusted name in whitewater rafting.

With a current of success behind them, Rapid Bonds Whitewater Rafting is proud to continue crafting unparalleled experiences for families and adventurers, helping them paddle their way to memories that will last a lifetime.
