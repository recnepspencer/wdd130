Our Trips:

Rapid Bonds

Experience "Rapid Bonds," our easy difficulty rafting trip down the serene Salmon River. Perfect for beginners and families, this gentle adventure offers stunning mountain vistas and wildlife sightings. Cruise along the tranquil currents, creating lasting bonds with your loved ones as you immerse yourself in nature's beauty. Disconnect from the everyday and embrace the joy of shared moments on this unforgettable rafting experience.

Rapid Chaos

Embark on the Rapid Chaos whitewater rafting trip down the Salmon River, offering medium-level difficulty and unforgettable thrills. Brave the untamed rapids, feeling the surge of adrenaline as you conquer challenging twists and turns. Immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty of the surrounding wilderness as you navigate through this exhilarating adventure. Prepare for heart-pounding moments and create lasting memories on this exciting whitewater rafting expedition.

Rapid Death

Prepare for the ultimate challenge with "Rapid Death," our most intense rafting experience reserved for the daring. Conquer the unforgiving rapids of the Salmon River at its most extreme difficulty level. Brace yourself as you navigate treacherous drops and powerful waves, testing your skills and pushing your limits. This adrenaline-fueled adventure promises heart-stopping excitement and a true test of courage for the fearless few who dare to embark on this thrilling and unforgettable journey.
